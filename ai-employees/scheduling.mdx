Scheduling sounds simple until you're three emails deep into finding a time that works, chasing a confirmation that never came back, or untangling a rescheduling chain the morning of the meeting. Your Jobr scheduling AI employee handles all of it — inbound requests, outbound booking, confirmations, reminders, rescheduling, and cancellations — so your team shows up to the meeting and never has to manage the logistics of getting there.

## What it handles

Your scheduling AI employee takes over every step of the booking workflow:

- **Inbound scheduling requests** — receives and responds to requests from clients, candidates, or anyone who needs to get on the calendar
- **Outbound scheduling** — reaches out to book time when your team needs to initiate a meeting
- **Confirmation messages** — sends booking confirmations the moment a meeting is set
- **Reminders** — follows up before the meeting to reduce no-shows
- **Rescheduling** — handles reschedule requests and finds a new time without involving your team
- **Cancellations** — processes cancellations, updates the calendar, and notifies the relevant parties

## Key benefits

- **Eliminates back-and-forth** — no more email threads to find a time that works
- **Reduces no-shows** — automated reminders keep meetings on the radar for everyone involved
- **Runs 24/7** — booking requests get handled the moment they come in, any time of day
- **Books into the right calendars** — connected directly to your team's calendars, so every booking lands in the right place

## What you configure

Jobr sets up your scheduling AI employee around your team's actual availability and booking rules:

- **Available time windows** — the hours and days your team is open for each meeting type
- **Meeting types and durations** — discovery calls, onboarding sessions, interviews, callbacks — each with its own rules
- **Reminder schedule** — when reminders go out and how many touchpoints before the meeting
- **Cancellation policy** — how the AI handles late cancellations and what it communicates to the other party

## Common use cases

The scheduling AI employee fits anywhere the back-and-forth of booking is costing your team time:

- **Client onboarding calls** — book new clients into the right session automatically after they sign
- **Sales discovery meetings** — combine with the lead response AI to book qualified leads directly
- **Support callbacks** — offer a scheduled callback instead of a queue and have the AI handle all the logistics
- **Interview scheduling** — coordinate availability between candidates and hiring managers without anyone playing calendar tag
