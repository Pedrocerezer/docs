Every minute a lead sits unanswered, the chance of converting them drops. Your Jobr lead response AI employee — an AI agent built for AI lead response & qualification (an AI SDR for your business) — replies to every inbound inquiry in seconds — around the clock, without fail. It then qualifies each lead against the criteria you define and books a meeting directly when the lead is a fit. No more slow follow-up. No more leads going cold while your team is tied up elsewhere.

## What it handles

Your lead response AI employee takes over the full top-of-funnel workflow:

- **Instant replies to every inbound inquiry** — web forms, emails, or any connected channel — within seconds of submission
- **Lead qualification** — asks the right questions and scores the lead against criteria you set
- **Meeting booking** — schedules directly into the right calendar when a lead qualifies
- **Routing complex inquiries** — passes leads that need a human conversation to your sales team with full context

## Key metrics

- Replies in under 30 seconds, every time
- Never misses an inquiry — nights, weekends, and peak volume included
- Works around the clock so no lead waits until Monday morning

## What you define

Jobr works with you during setup to configure everything the AI employee needs to qualify the right leads for your business:

- **Qualification criteria** — company size, budget range, industry, use case, or any combination that defines a fit for you
- **Tone and messaging** — how the AI employee introduces itself and communicates on your behalf
- **What "qualified" means** — the threshold that triggers a meeting booking versus a handoff to sales
- **Meeting booking rules** — which calendar it books into, available time windows, and meeting types

<Tip>
  Define tight qualification criteria upfront — the more specific you are about what a good lead looks like, the more effectively the AI filters for you. Vague criteria produce vague results.
</Tip>

## How a typical inquiry flows

Here is what happens from the moment a lead submits a form or sends a message:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Inquiry comes in">
    A prospect submits a form, sends an email, or reaches out through a connected channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI replies instantly">
    Your lead response AI employee responds within seconds with a personalised message in your brand voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Qualification questions">
    The AI asks the questions that matter — budget, company size, timeline, use case — based on the criteria you defined.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meeting booked or routed">
    If the lead qualifies, the AI books a meeting directly. If the situation is complex or the lead needs a human touch, it routes to your sales team with full context.
  </Step>
</Steps>
