Manual invoice processing is one of the most expensive invisible costs in a growing business. Every invoice that gets keyed in by hand, every PO match done by eye, every discrepancy that sits in someone's queue — it adds up in hours and in errors. Your Jobr invoice processing AI employee takes over the entire workflow: it reads incoming invoices, matches them to the right purchase orders or contracts, routes them for approval, and flags anything that doesn't add up. Your team stops touching routine invoices and starts handling only the exceptions.

## What it handles

Your invoice processing AI employee takes over the repetitive work in your accounts payable workflow:

- **Reading incoming invoices** — from email, PDF, or any connected source
- **Matching to purchase orders or contracts** — automatically pairs each invoice to the right record
- **Routing for approval** — sends invoices to the right person based on your approval rules
- **Flagging discrepancies** — catches mismatches in amounts, line items, or supplier details before they become problems

## Key benefits

- **No manual entry** — invoices are read and processed automatically, without anyone typing a line
- **No backlog** — invoices are handled as they arrive, not when someone gets to them
- **Instant processing** — the workflow moves at the speed of the AI, not the speed of the queue
- **Consistent accuracy** — the same matching and flagging rules applied every time, without fatigue

## What you configure

Jobr maps your existing invoice workflow before building your AI employee. During that process, you define:

- **Matching rules** — which invoices match to which purchase orders, contracts, or supplier records
- **Approval thresholds** — the invoice amounts or conditions that require a human to sign off
- **Escalation triggers** — what gets flagged and who it goes to when something doesn't match

<Note>
  Jobr maps your existing invoice workflow before building — the AI employee follows your process, not a generic one. If you have rules that are unique to your business, they get built in.
</Note>

## What it doesn't replace

Final approval decisions above your configured threshold always route to a human. Your Jobr invoice processing AI employee handles the work — reading, matching, routing, flagging — but it never signs off on something you've told it to escalate. Anything above your approval threshold lands in the right person's queue with full context, ready for a quick decision.
