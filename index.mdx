## How Jobr gets you from problem to working AI employee

The entire process — from scoping the role to the AI employee handling real work — takes weeks, not months. Here's how it unfolds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Map the role">
    Jobr learns exactly how the work happens inside your business today — the process, the tone, the rules, and the edge cases that matter. This becomes the foundation your AI employee is built on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your AI employee">
    Jobr custom-builds your AI employee around your operation. It's trained on your process and bounded by the guardrails you approve — not a generic template pulled off a shelf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Jobr deploys and runs the employee. It starts handling real work, escalates what matters to a human, and reports back on everything it did. Your team moves on.
  </Step>
</Steps>
