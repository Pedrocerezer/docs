Jobr is a done-for-you AI employee service. Jobr builds, deploys, and manages custom AI workers that take over specific, repetitive roles in your business — the kind of work that's eating your team's time and your company's money. You don't buy software, manage a platform, or train anything yourself. Jobr handles all of it, and the AI employee shows up ready to work.

## The problem Jobr solves

Repetitive, high-volume work is everywhere in business — and it's expensive in ways that are easy to ignore until you add them up.

The same customer questions answered hundreds of times a week. Leads going cold because no one got back to them fast enough. Invoices entered by hand, one line at a time. Overdue payments no one got around to chasing. These tasks are not complex, but they demand constant attention. They pull your best people away from the work only humans can do, and they're nearly impossible to scale without just hiring more headcount.

Jobr exists to take that work off your plate — permanently, reliably, and without adding to your management load.

## What a Jobr AI employee actually is

A Jobr AI employee is not a chatbot you configure yourself. It's not a generic automation tool you buy a subscription to and then figure out on your own. It's a purpose-built AI worker, designed around how your specific business operates and deployed by Jobr's team.

Every AI employee is:

- **Custom-built for your workflow.** Jobr maps the role before building anything — learning your process, your tone, your rules, and the edge cases that matter in your business.
- **Done-for-you from start to finish.** You don't learn a platform or manage a build. Jobr does the scoping, building, deployment, and ongoing management.
- **Available 24/7.** Your AI employee doesn't clock out, call in sick, or go on holiday. It works around the clock, every day.
- **Bounded by guardrails you control.** You set the limits. The AI employee works within them, escalates what matters to a human, and reports back on everything it handled.

## What Jobr is not

It's worth being clear about what Jobr does not offer:

- **Not a self-serve tool.** There's no platform to log into and configure. Jobr builds and runs everything for you.
- **Not a chatbot.** Jobr AI employees are purpose-built for a specific operational role — not a general-purpose chat interface bolted onto your website.
- **Not a one-size-fits-all product.** Every AI employee is scoped and built specifically for your business and your workflows.
